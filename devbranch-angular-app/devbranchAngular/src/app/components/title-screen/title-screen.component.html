About the Story

This is an interactive murder mystery game set in an enterprise datacenter. You play as an investigator tasked with solving three murders and unmasking the perpetrator. Collect clues, navigate the facility, interview suspects, and piece together the evidence to reach the truth.

Mode selection happens after you enter the game. Easy Mode: starts with pre-collected clues for a faster run. Normal Mode: collect all clues yourself for the full investigative experience.
